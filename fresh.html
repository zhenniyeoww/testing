Did You Know?

Fresh fragrances capture the essence of cleanliness, water, and air—drawing inspiration from oceans, rain, and cool breezes. This scent category gained popularity in the late 20th century with advances in synthetic notes like ozonic and aquatic accords. Often paired with green, fruity, or citrusy touches, fresh scents are light, energizing, and modern—ideal for those who love a clean, breezy, and effortless vibe that suits any season.
