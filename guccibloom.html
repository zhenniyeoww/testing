Gucci Bloom

Country: Italy
Gucci Bloom is a Floral fragrance for women, launched in 2017. Created by Alessandro Michele and perfumer Alberto Morillas, this fragrance is a celebration of florals. The composition is centered around the notes of tuberose, jasmine, and Rangoon creeper, a unique flower from India. The scent is lush, feminine, and intoxicating, making it ideal for women who enjoy heady floral fragrances. Gucci Bloom is a beautiful, classic floral fragrance that works well for daily wear or special occasions.
