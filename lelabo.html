Le Labo Gaiac 10

Country: USA
Le Labo Gaiac 10 is a Woody fragrance that is unisex, released in 2008. This fragrance is inspired by the scent of gaiac wood, a rare and precious ingredient. The opening is fresh and spicy with lemon and rosemary, leading into a heart of gaiac wood and cedar. The fragrance is rounded out by musk, providing a soft, skin-like warmth. Gaiac 10 is a minimalistic yet sophisticated fragrance, ideal for those who enjoy woody scents with a touch of freshness. Buy Now
