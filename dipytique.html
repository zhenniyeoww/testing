Diptyque Tam Dao

Country: France
Diptyque Tam Dao is a Woody fragrance for both men and women, introduced in 2003. Inspired by the forests of Indochina, this fragrance highlights the creamy, rich note of sandalwood. The opening is fresh with a hint of cypress and myrtle, while the heart reveals the warm, soothing qualities of sandalwood. The base includes cedar and spices, which deepen the fragrance and enhance its woodiness. Tam Dao is a tranquil and serene fragrance, perfect for those who enjoy a clean, woody scent.
