Narciso Rodriguez For Him Bleu Noir

Country: France
Narciso Rodriguez for Him Blue Noir is a Woody Floral Musk fragrance for men, launched in 2015. It features an elegant balance of floral, woody, and musky notes. The fragrance opens with a fresh and cool combination of musk, amber, and violet leaf, which creates an intriguing contrast to the woody heart. Cedarwood and patchouli enhance the depth of the fragrance, while the base notes of vetiver and tonka bean add warmth and richness. Narciso Rodriguez for Him Blue Noir is a versatile fragrance that exudes masculinity and sophistication, perfect for both day and night wear.
