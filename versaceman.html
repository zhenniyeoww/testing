Versace Man Eau Fraîche

Country: Italy
Versace Man Eau Fraîche is a Fresh Aquatic Woody fragrance for men, released in 2006 as a lighter, more refreshing version of the original Versace Man. It opens with invigorating top notes of lemon, bergamot, and star fruit, creating a sparkling citrus burst. The heart introduces a unique blend of tarragon, sage, and cedarwood, giving it an aromatic and slightly herbal edge. At its base, the scent settles into a clean, masculine trail of musk, sycamore wood, and amber, leaving a soft, sensual impression. With its cool, breezy character, Versace Man Eau Fraîche is perfect for casual wear and summer days—exuding confidence, style, and Mediterranean charm.
