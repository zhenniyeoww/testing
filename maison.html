Maison Francis Kurkdjian Aqua Universalis

Country: France
Maison Francis Kurkdjian Aqua Universalis is a Citrus Floral fragrance for men and women, released in 2009. It is a versatile and luminous fragrance that captures the essence of freshness and cleanliness. The fragrance opens with bright lemon and bergamot, complemented by a soft, floral heart of lily of the valley and orange blossom. The base of musk adds a clean, skin-like finish, making it a perfect everyday scent. Aqua Universalis is a refreshing and light fragrance that works well in any season, ideal for those who enjoy clean and citrus-forward scents.
