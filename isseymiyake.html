Issey Miyake L’Eau d’Issey

Country: Japan
Issey Miyake L’Eau d’Issey is a Fresh Floral Aquatic fragrance introduced in 1992. It captures the essence of water in a refined, feminine form. The scent opens with a fresh blend of lotus, melon, and freesia, creating a clean, dew-like impression. At its heart are delicate floral notes of lily and peony, contributing softness and elegance. The base of sandalwood, cedar, and musk grounds the fragrance with a serene, slightly woody undertone. Known for its purity and balance, this perfume is ideal for women who appreciate minimalist sophistication with a hint of natural serenity.
