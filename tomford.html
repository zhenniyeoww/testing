Tom Ford Oud Wood

Country: USA
Tom Ford Oud Wood is a luxurious and complex fragrance from the Woody Oriental family, designed for both men and women (leans masculine). Launched in 2007, this scent is a refined exploration of oud, a rare and prized ingredient in perfumery. The fragrance opens with the smoky richness of oud wood, combined with the warm, spicy notes of cardamom and Brazilian rosewood. The heart is enhanced by the creamy, soft sandalwood, while the base reveals subtle hints of vetiver, tonka bean, and amber. Tom Ford Oud Wood is an exotic, sophisticated scent, perfect for those who appreciate bold yet elegant fragrances.
