Did You Know?

Floral fragrances are the heart of perfumery, tracing back to ancient times when flowers were pressed and distilled into oils for royalty and rituals. In the 19th century, the art of floral blending flourished in France with iconic scents like rose, jasmine, and lily becoming symbols of femininity, grace, and romance. Whether soft and powdery or bold and exotic, floral perfumes remain timeless, evoking emotion, memory, and beauty with every bloom-inspired note.
