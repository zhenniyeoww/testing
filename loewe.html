Loewe Solo Cedro

Country: France
Loewe Soli Cedro is a Woody fragrance for both men and women, released in 2016. This fragrance is inspired by the fresh, invigorating scent of cedarwood, which serves as its dominant note. The fragrance opens with zesty lemon and bergamot, providing a bright and refreshing introduction. As it develops, the woody cedar note takes center stage, balanced by the aromatic spiciness of rosemary and a touch of amber. Loewe Soli Cedro is a crisp, clean scent with a timeless quality, ideal for those who enjoy fresh yet woody fragrances.
