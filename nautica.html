Nautica Voyage

Country: USA
Nautica Voyage is a Fresh Aquatic fragrance for men, launched in 2006. With its clean, crisp, and invigorating scent, it evokes the feeling of a fresh ocean breeze. The fragrance opens with bright and zesty notes of green apple and water lotus, giving it a refreshing and youthful vibe. The heart features mimosa and lotus flower, while the base adds depth with musk, cedarwood, and amber. Nautica Voyage is a perfect fragrance for casual wear and is especially ideal for the warmer months when you want a light, refreshing scent.
