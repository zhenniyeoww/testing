Terre d’Hermès

Country: France
Terre d’Hermès is a Woody Spicy fragrance for men, released in 2006. Created by perfumer Jean-Claude Ellena, it represents the essence of the earth and sky, blending citrus notes of orange and grapefruit with a peppery, woody heart. The fragrance's base is anchored by vetiver, cedar, and benzoin, providing a grounding and earthy finish. Terre d’Hermès is a fragrance that evokes nature's raw beauty, combining freshness with a deep, woody richness. Its versatility and refinement make it a timeless classic in the men's fragrance world.
